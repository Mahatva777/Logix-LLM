# Reliance Industries — Fundamentals (FY26 / Q4 FY26)

SOURCE: Curated from Kotak Neo's Reliance Industries Q4 FY26 results coverage, ticker.finology market-update analysis, and RIL's official financial reporting (ril.com).

DATE: FY26 full year and Q4 FY26 (quarter ended March 31, 2026)

## Full-Year Performance

FY26 is being framed as a record year for Reliance Industries, with gross revenue of approximately ₹11.76 lakh crore and profit after tax of around ₹95,700 crore for the full year. Strong contributions from the Jio and Retail businesses were the key drivers behind this record performance, underscoring the company's diversification beyond its traditional energy base.

## Q4 FY26 Performance

Despite the strong full-year picture, Q4 FY26 profit after tax dipped sequentially, with figures cited in the ₹20,500–28,500 crore range depending on the source, even as quarterly revenue remained strong. This sequential profit decline is attributed to pressure in the energy and O2C (oil-to-chemicals) segment along with higher expenses, rather than to weakness in Jio or Retail.

## Capital Expenditure

The company continues to run at high investment intensity, with FY26 capex exceeding ₹1.44 lakh crore and Q4 FY26 capex alone at around ₹40,560 crore. This level of ongoing capex is a relevant balance-sheet consideration, since it implies continued heavy reinvestment even during a quarter of sequential profit softness.
