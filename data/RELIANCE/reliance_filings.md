# Reliance Industries — Filings & Corporate Disclosures

SOURCE: Curated from RIL's official financial reporting (ril.com) and Economic Times coverage of RIL AGM 2026 preview.

DATE: FY26 capex disclosures; AGM 2026 (upcoming, per Economic Times preview)

## Capex Disclosure

RIL's official financial reporting discloses FY26 capital expenditure exceeding ₹1.44 lakh crore, with Q4 FY26 capex alone at approximately ₹40,560 crore. This level of disclosed investment intensity is relevant to balance-sheet and free-cash-flow analysis, as it indicates the company is continuing to invest heavily even in a quarter where profit dipped sequentially due to energy-segment pressure.

## Segment Margin Pressure

Company and analyst commentary tied to the Q4 FY26 results filing identifies margin pressure in the energy and O2C segment as the primary explanation for the sequential profit dip, even as consolidated revenue hit record levels. This distinguishes the quarter's softness as segment-specific rather than reflecting weakness across the group's other businesses.

## Upcoming AGM 2026 Catalysts

Forward-looking coverage frames the upcoming AGM 2026 as a venue for several potential catalysts and execution-risk items, including updates on a possible Jio Platforms IPO, retail expansion plans, and a new energy roadmap. These are disclosed as anticipated discussion points rather than confirmed outcomes, and represent execution risk to the extent that any of these initiatives slip or underdeliver relative to market expectations.
