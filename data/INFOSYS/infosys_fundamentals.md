# Infosys — Fundamentals (Q4 FY26)

SOURCE: Curated from Infosys's official quarterly results page (infosys.com), Tickeron's Q4 FY26 earnings preview, and Economic Times live coverage of Infosys Q4 results.

DATE: 2026-04 (Q4 FY26 results reporting window)

## Profitability and Shareholder Returns

Infosys reported net profit of approximately ₹8,501 crore for Q4 FY26, up around 21% year-on-year, alongside revenue growth for the quarter. The board also declared a final dividend of ₹25 per share, signalling continued confidence in cash generation even against a more measured demand backdrop.

## Guidance

Management raised or reiterated growth and margin guidance for FY26/27 within a moderate range. Specifically, FY27 revenue guidance was set at roughly 1.5–3.5% constant-currency growth, a relatively muted band that analysts have read as reflecting soft demand and cautious client spending rather than aggressive expansion plans.

## AI Strategy and Long-Term Opportunity

Infosys has been actively positioning itself around an AI-first strategy, marketed through initiatives such as Infosys Topaz and events like the Investor AI Day and India AI Summit. Company leadership, including Nilekani, has pointed to a long-term AI services opportunity estimated at USD 300–400 billion by 2030, positioning AI as a structural growth vector well beyond the current guidance period.
