# ICICI Bank — News & Market Commentary

SOURCE: Curated from Moneycontrol coverage citing Motilal Oswal research, and StockGro blog analysis of ICICI Bank Q4 FY26 results.

## Q4 FY26 Results Reaction

DATE: 2026-04
SENTIMENT: positive

Coverage of ICICI Bank's Q4 FY26 results was clearly positive-leaning, highlighting profit after tax growth of around 8.5% year-on-year to approximately ₹13,702 crore, alongside strong loan growth and improving asset quality metrics including gross NPA near 1.40% and net NPA as low as 0.33%.

## Brokerage Positioning

DATE: 2026-04
SENTIMENT: positive

Motilal Oswal's commentary, as covered by Moneycontrol, named ICICI Bank a top pick among lenders for the period, citing robust asset quality and healthy provisioning as the key supporting factors. This reflects strong sell-side confidence in the bank's near-term fundamentals relative to peers.

## Margin and Rate-Cycle Commentary

DATE: 2026-04
SENTIMENT: neutral

StockGro's analysis described net interest margin, at around 4.32%, as stable overall, while still noting mild pressure from interest-rate transmission working through the book. The tone here is measured rather than alarmed, framing rate-cycle sensitivity as a manageable, ongoing watch-item rather than an active concern.
