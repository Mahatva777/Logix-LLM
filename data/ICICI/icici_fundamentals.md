# ICICI Bank — Fundamentals (Q4 FY26)

SOURCE: Curated from Moneycontrol coverage citing Motilal Oswal research, and StockGro blog analysis of ICICI Bank Q4 FY26 results.

DATE: 2026-04 (Q4 FY26 results reporting window)

## Profitability

ICICI Bank reported profit after tax of approximately ₹13,702 crore for Q4 FY26, up around 8.5% year-on-year. This growth was supported by strong loan growth during the quarter, pointing to healthy underlying business momentum rather than one-off gains.

## Net Interest Margin

Net interest margin stood at around 4.32% for the quarter. Commentary describes this margin as broadly stable, though it also flags mild pressure from interest-rate transmission effects working through the book, meaning the margin picture is healthy today but carries some sensitivity to the ongoing rate cycle.

## Asset Quality

Asset quality metrics improved further, with gross NPA at around 1.40% and net NPA as low as 0.33%. Brokerage commentary, including from Motilal Oswal, has cited this combination of robust asset quality and healthy provisioning as the basis for naming ICICI Bank a top pick among lenders for the period.
