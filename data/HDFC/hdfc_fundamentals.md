# HDFC Bank — Fundamentals (Q4 FY26)

SOURCE: Curated from ticker.finology market-update analysis, JM Financial Services blog coverage of HDFC Bank Q4 FY26 results, and Reuters reporting on HDFC Bank's Q4 FY26 profit beat.

DATE: 2026-04-18 (Q4 FY26 results reporting window)

## Profitability

HDFC Bank reported net profit of approximately ₹19,221 crore for Q4 FY26, up around 9.1% year-on-year. Net interest income growth for the quarter was in the low single digits, a comparatively modest pace that stood in contrast to the healthier bottom-line growth, suggesting other levers such as fee income or provisioning were doing some of the work on profit.

## Net Interest Margin

Net interest margin came in at around 3.38% on total assets for the quarter, with some reports citing a higher figure of about 3.53% when measured on interest-earning assets specifically. Either way, coverage consistently frames this as structurally below the roughly 4% margin the bank ran at before its merger, with funding mix and merger integration cited as ongoing drags.

## Balance Sheet Quality

Deposit growth for the quarter was described as strong, a positive signal for funding stability. Asset quality also improved, with gross NPA at around 1.15%, indicating the loan book remains healthy even as margins compress. Taken together, the quarter reads as operationally resilient on volume and asset quality, with margin recovery as the key open question.
