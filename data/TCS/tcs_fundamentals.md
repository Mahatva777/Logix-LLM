# TCS — Fundamentals (Q4 FY26)

SOURCE: Curated from ticker.finology market-update analysis and Multibagg market-pulse coverage of TCS Q4 FY26 results, and Ventura Securities research note on TCS Q4 results.

DATE: 2026-04-14 (Q4 FY26 results reporting window)

## Revenue and Profit

TCS reported consolidated revenue of ₹70,698 crore for Q4 FY26, marking a strong sequential improvement for the quarter. Consolidated net profit for the same quarter came in at ₹13,718 crore, which represented a rise of around 29% quarter-on-quarter and about 12% year-on-year.

## Margins

Operating margin for the quarter stood at 25.3%, while net margin was reported at 19.4%. Commentary around the results emphasized margin discipline as a key theme, suggesting the company held cost efficiency steady even as revenue growth remained a mixed picture across segments.

## Growth Quality and AI Business

While headline quarterly numbers were strong, analysts also pointed to soft constant-currency growth for the full year, indicating that reported growth was flattered in part by currency effects rather than being uniformly broad-based in underlying business terms. On the positive side, TCS's AI-linked revenue run-rate was highlighted as having crossed roughly USD 2.3 billion, positioning artificial intelligence as an increasingly material growth vector for the company rather than a side initiative.

## Macro Context

Coverage of the quarter repeatedly noted that global macroeconomic uncertainty and geopolitical headwinds remain a live constraint on the company's growth outlook, even though the Q4 print itself was read as broadly encouraging.
